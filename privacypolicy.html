Privacy policy

This privacy policy sets how Your Lucky Code uses and protects information that you give it when you use this website.

Your Lucky Code is committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected. Should we ask you to provide certain information by which you can be identified when using this website, then you can be assured that it will only be used in accordance with this privacy policy.


Your Lucky Code may change this policy from time to time by updating this page. You should check this page from time to time to ensure that you are happy with any changes. This policy is effective from 1st September 2018.



Information We Collect


We may collect the following information when you fill in our enquiry form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Company name
  • Project type
  • Budget
  • Project information

What We Do With This Information

Your Lucky Code requires this information to understand your needs and provide you with a better service, and in particular for the following reasons:


Internal record keeping

YLC may use the information to improve its products and services. It may periodically send you emails about new products, special offers or other information that it thinks you may find interesting using the email address which you have provided. From time to time, it may also use your information to contact you for market research purposes. It may contact you by email, phone, or mail. It may use the information to customize the website according to your interests.


Security

YLC is committed to ensuring that your information is secure. In order to prevent unauthorised access or disclosure, it has put in place suitable physical, electronic and managerial procedures to safeguard and secure the information it collects online.


Google Analytics

YLC uses traffic log cookies from Google Analytics to identify which pages are being used and is an essential part of monitoring and improving its electronic communications. This helps it analyse data about web page traffic and improve its website in order to tailor it to customer needs. YLC uses this non-identifiable aggregated traffic information for statistical analysis purposes.


Other Applications

If YLC embeds YouTube videos in the site, watching the video will set a cookie on your computer. YLC may also use embedded applications such as Twitter or Facebook. These third parties may set cookies to remember your preferences.


Controlling Your Personal Information

You may choose to restrict the collection or use of your personal information in the following ways: Whenever you are asked to fill in a form on the website, look for the box that you can click to indicate that you do not want the information to be used by anybody for direct marketing purposes. If you have previously agreed to YLC using your personal information for direct marketing purposes, you may change your mind at any time by writing to or emailing us at yourluckycode@gmail.com. YLC will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless it has your permission or are required by law to do so. YLC may use your personal information to send you promotional information about third parties which we think you may find interesting if you tell us that you wish this to happen.


You may request details of personal information which YLC holds about you under the Data Protection Act 1998. A small fee will be payable. If you would like a copy of the information held on you please write to yourluckycode@gmail.com.


If you believe that any information YLC is holding on you is incorrect or incomplete, please write to or email us as soon as possible, at the above address. YLC will promptly correct any information found to be incorrect.
