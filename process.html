7 STEPS TO DELIVER EXTRAORDINARY PRODUCT, and why I believe they are our NICHE!

1. Let's talk

Encounter with a cliet, usually a conversation about your startup, company, bussiness, idea and needs. We can meet in person if you live in NYC, but if you don't, there is skype. I am open to work with clients around the globe.

2. Information Search

I am gathering the information about the genre of your business. I try to analyze your competitiors, see how they do the things, apply better solutions to your project and make you stand out of the crowd.

3. Technical Decision

I am deciding upon the technologies, frameworks, libraries and effects that I am going to use in order to build your website. I am using technologies which suit the best your needs at the current moment.

4. Project Architecture

Project Architecture is the process when I get things done on the paper. I create design layout, web content, social media kit, I choose the right colors, fonts, hosting service and technologies for your business.

5. Official Presentation

I am presenting you the project architecture, I am open for changes and suggestions. It is your startup, business, brand. I want you to be a part of it and feel it looks the way you have always dreamt of.

6. Design & Development

Process of coding! At this point, I still accept small changes of design, your website must look the way you want. My websites are usually hand-coded, but I also use CMS if it is a better solution for your bussiness.

7. Product Delivery

Project is delivered to you! I am paying attention to every detail making sure that the final product is polished. Your satissfaction and aprobation have the highest value for me.
